Hospital capacity

Most recent data: Apr 16, 2021 – Apr 22, 2021

In the week ending Apr 22, 2021, there were 134 patients hospitalized with confirmed or suspected cases of Covid-19 in Westchester hospitals (9% of all inpatients). 66% of all inpatient beds were occupied and 53% of all ICU beds were occupied. There were 755 total beds free and 121 ICU beds free. Statewide, 74% of all inpatient beds were occupied and 68% of all ICU beds were occupied.

All hospital metrics reported are weekly averages.

Westchester Medical Center, the largest hospital in the county, did not report complete data before mid-Oct 2020.
