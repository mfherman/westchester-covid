Hospital capacity

Most recent data: Jan 8, 2021 Jan 14, 2021

In the week ending Jan 14, 2021, there were 477 patients hospitalized with confirmed or suspected cases of Covid-19 in Westchester hospitals (28% of all inpatients). 74% of all inpatient beds were occupied and 61% of all ICU beds were occupied. There were 582 total beds free and 100 ICU beds free. Statewide, 75% of all inpatient beds were occupied and 70% of all ICU beds were occupied.

All hospital metrics reported are weekly averages.

Westchester Medical Center, the largest hospital in the county, did not report complete data before mid-Oct 2020.
