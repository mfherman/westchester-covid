Hospital capacity

Most recent data: Jul 29, 2022 Aug 4, 2022

In the week ending Aug 4, 2022, there were 149 patients hospitalized with confirmed or suspected cases of Covid-19 in Westchester hospitals (9% of all inpatients). 79% of all inpatient beds were occupied and 73% of all ICU beds were occupied. There were 424 total beds free and 51 ICU beds free. Statewide, 80% of all inpatient beds were occupied and 71% of all ICU beds were occupied.

All hospital metrics reported are weekly averages.

Westchester Medical Center, the largest hospital in the county, did not report complete data before mid-Oct 2020.
